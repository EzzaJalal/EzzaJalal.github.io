The human body is a complex, highly organized structure made up of unique cells that work together to accomplish the specific functions necessary for sustaining life, to tissues and organs. The cells in a tissue are not identical, but they work together to accomplish specific functions. There is no doubt about it, the human body is complex, perhaps the most complex machine on earth. This is why body donation is crucial to the practice of medicine. It is critical for surgeons to educate themselves and to be well practiced on all surgical procedures, both complicated and routine.
