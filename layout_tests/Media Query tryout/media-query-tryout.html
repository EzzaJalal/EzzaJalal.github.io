About

Finland, situated in Northern Europe, is known for its stunning natural landscapes, including thousands of lakes, dense forests, and the magical Northern Lights. It's a country with a rich cultural heritage, blending Nordic and indigenous Sami traditions. The Finnish people are known for their resilience, innovation, and love for nature, making Finland a unique and welcoming destination for visitors and residents alike.
